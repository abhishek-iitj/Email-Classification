Why are we letting you know?
Our security system detected that you haven’t logged in from this location before. It may simply be that your network configuration changed or you logged in from a new physical location. It could also mean somebody else has accessed your account.

Don’t recognize this activity?
Log in to your account and go to Settings > Account settings > Security > Audit log. If you are seeing unfamiliar locations on the list, we recommend changing your password.

Want to add extra security to your account?
Try LastPass Authenticator, LogMeIn's own two-factor authenticator app, to add an additional layer of security against unauthorized access.
